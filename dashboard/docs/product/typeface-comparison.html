Quick Comparison

Monospace

Pros:
• Most technical/industrial
• Code editor feel
• Precise, engineered

Cons:
• Less refined at large sizes
• Can feel dated
• Lower readability

Inter (Modern Sans)

Pros:
• Most readable & professional
• Works at all sizes
• Calm, approachable

Cons:
• "Safe" choice, less distinctive
• Maybe less "industrial"
• Overused in design

Space Grotesk

Pros:
• Engineered + clean balance
• Distinctive, not overused
• Geometric precision

Cons:
• Slightly less readable
• Needs good spacing
• Less common in dev tools
